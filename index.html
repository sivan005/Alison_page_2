What is Alison Premium Monthly?

You asked, we’ve listened... Over the years, many of our learners have given us suggestions for ways we could improve your learning journey on Alison. Taking on board all those wonderful ideas from our worldwide community, we are proud and excited to introduce Alison Premium Monthly, a brand new learning experience for Alison learners.

So what is Premium Monthly exactly? It’s the same Alison you love, with over 1,000 free courses, but with absolutely no ads on courses or the entire Alison site. What’s more, Premium Monthly also gives you exclusive monthly discounts that you can use towards any Certificate or Diploma available to you. The discounts you receive will not expire, so you can redeem them whenever you want to. You can also save your discounts and get up to 50% off one Certificate or Diploma. The best part? You can cancel your subscription anytime!
